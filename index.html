Bill Gates's Life History:

  • 1955-Bill Gates was born on October 28,1955.
  • 1967-Bill Gates Enrolled in Lakeside school, an exclusive preparatory school.
  • 1973-Gates enrolled at Harvard, where he studied mathematics and computer science.
  • 1976-Bill Gates co-founded Microsoft when he formed a contract with MITS (Micro Instrumentation and Telemetry Systems) to develop a basic operating system for their new microcomputers.
  • 1981-Bill Gates became the President of Microsoft.
  • 1989-Bill Gates founded Corbis.
  • 1990- Microsoft released its first version of Windows.
  • 1992-Bill Gates is married to Melinda French.
  • 1995-In age 39 with the fortune of 12.8 billion, Gates becomes the world's richest man.
  • 2008-Bill Gates stepped down from everything.
